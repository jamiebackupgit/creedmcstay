Litigation

With over 35 years of experience, our litigation team has a proven track record of representing individuals, families, and businesses in some of the most complex and high-profile cases to come before the Courts and Statutory Forums. Our focus is on asserting and protecting your interests by procuring resolutions that effectively and decisively conclude disputes in your favour.

Taxation Appeals

We have extensive expertise in representing clients before the Tax Appeals Commission in disputes involving determinations from the Revenue Commissioners and the Criminal Assets Bureau. Our team has played a pivotal role in landmark cases, safeguarding taxpayer rights and challenging state actions that overstep the boundaries of the tax code. With our experience, we ensure your rights are rigorously defended in this specialised area.

Property

Our property team has represented clients in some of the most significant property transactions in the state. We provide comprehensive legal support to a diverse range of stakeholders, including developers, homeowners, commercial landlords and tenants, lenders, and borrowers. Whether it’s acquisition, development, financing, mortgaging, leasing, or property management, we offer expert advice tailored to your unique needs.

Commercial and Corporate

We deliver strategic advice and legal support to businesses at every stage of their journey, from start-ups to established enterprises. Our expertise spans shareholder agreements, security, funding, governance, and the acquisition or disposal of assets and shares. With our guidance, your business will have the legal foundation it needs to grow and thrive.

Debt Restructure

Our team works closely with all major financial institutions to assist clients in resolving and restructuring their financial affairs. For those facing financial difficulty, we consistently secure the most favourable outcomes, leveraging our exceptional track record in crafting innovative solutions that others may overlook. Even in the most challenging cases, we excel at finding paths to resolution.
